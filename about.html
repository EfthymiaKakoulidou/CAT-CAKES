About Me


Hej! I am a self-taught hobby cake creator based in Stockholm love to give children joy with a wish-cake! I am very passionate about baking cakes whether they are based on your ideas or come from my creative process. When I am not baking cakes I do research and teach archeology at the University of Stockholm.
